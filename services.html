Services

I help organizations, researchers, and public health teams transform raw data into actionable insights. Below are the service packages I offer. Each package is designed to match the needs of different types of projects, from data cleaning and descriptive summaries to advanced modeling and ongoing consultation.

Foundations

Best for: small nonprofits, educators, or early-stage researchers needing structured data.

  • Data cleaning & standardization (Excel, CSV, survey data)
  • Basic descriptive statistics (counts, averages, charts)
  • Simple data visualizations & reports
  • 30-minute consultation call

Starting at $250–500

Insights

Best for: teams seeking actionable findings from interventions and surveys.

  • Everything in Foundations
  • Comparative testing (t-tests, chi-square, ANOVA, etc.)
  • Pre/post intervention analysis
  • Comprehensive written report with visuals
  • 1-hour live walkthrough & Q&A

Starting at $750–1,500

Analytics

Best for: academic, policy, or research groups working with complex datasets.

  • Everything in Insights
  • Regression, logistic, & multilevel modeling
  • Bootstrapping & resampling methods
  • Machine learning models (predictive & classification)
  • Technical appendix with reproducible code
  • Strategic 1–2 hour consultation session

Starting at $2,000–5,000+

Consulting Retainer

Best for: organizations or researchers needing ongoing statistical support.

  • Monthly retainer (flexible hours)
  • Data consulting & project advising
  • Live problem-solving sessions
  • Quick turnaround data checks

$500–1,500/month
